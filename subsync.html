SubSync App Design

SubSync

SubSync is a subscription manager app that helps users manage all their subscriptions in one convenient place. Simple, intuitive, and user-friendly interface for effortless tracking.

Key Features

  • Centralized subscription tracking for multiple services.
  • Notifications for upcoming payments and renewals.
  • Intuitive dashboard for monitoring subscription costs.
  • Simple and clean UI/UX designed for easy navigation.
