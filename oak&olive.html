What is OakOlive?

OakOlive is a modern e-commerce platform built around sustainability. The goal was to design a shopping experience that feels clean, trustworthy, and easy to navigate — making eco-friendly products accessible to everyone.

Homepage

The homepage introduces the brand with a clean hero section, featured product categories, and a clear call to action.

Product Page

Product pages are designed to give users all the information they need — images, description, eco-credentials, and an easy add-to-cart flow.
